LOCATION

  • Sabangan is situated in Barangay Bangcud, about 15 to 20 minutes from Malaybalay City proper. Travelers heading to Sabangan can ride a jeepney or habal-habal from Malaybalay City proper and follow the road leading to Barangay Can-ayan. From there, they can continue toward the highland area until they reach the scenic hills and river spot of Sabangan.


ACTIVITIES

  • Camping on open hills: Set up tents, relax under the stars, and enjoy the cool mountain air.
  • Hiking and trail walks: Explore rolling landscapes, gentle slopes, and scenic viewpoints.
  • River-play and light swimming: Cool off in the shallow, refreshing water areas.
  • Sunrise and sunset viewing: Capture golden-hour colors over the hills.
